Associate — Intellectual Property
IP 2–4 years experience

About the Role

Join our IP department handling trademark and patent prosecution, enforcement, and portfolio management for multinational clients. You'll work directly with brand owners on registrations, oppositions, and customs enforcement across Thailand.

Requirements

  • Thai lawyer's licence (or eligible to obtain one)
  • 2–4 years' experience in intellectual property law
  • Strong English drafting and communication skills
  • Experience with DIP procedures and CIPITC litigation preferred
  • Familiarity with Madrid Protocol and PCT procedures is a plus

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary with annual review
  • CPD budget and conference attendance (INTA, APAA)
  • Cross-practice exposure across all departments
  • Clear pathway to Senior Associate and beyond

Senior Associate — Immigration
Immigration 4–6 years experience

About the Role

Lead our growing immigration practice — managing work permit and visa applications for corporate clients, coordinating LTR Visa submissions as a BOI Certified Agency, and mentoring junior team members. You'll handle a portfolio of multinational employers and high-net-worth individuals.

Requirements

  • Thai lawyer's licence
  • 4–6 years' experience in Thai immigration law
  • Proven track record managing high-volume corporate immigration accounts
  • Fluent English (written and spoken)
  • Experience with BOI processes and LTR Visa applications strongly preferred

What We Offer

  • Senior-level compensation package
  • Direct client relationship management
  • Leadership opportunities within the immigration department
  • International network access through ILN and ASEAN Attorneys Alliance
