Practice Area

Intellectual Property

Our IP Department handles every stage of intellectual property protection in Thailand and across the region — from initial filing and registration through to enforcement, licensing, and commercialisation. We act for multinational brand owners, technology companies, pharmaceutical groups, and FMCG businesses managing portfolios that span multiple jurisdictions and asset classes.

Trademark

We advise on the full lifecycle of trademark protection under Thai law: clearance searches, filing, prosecution, opposition and cancellation proceedings, renewals, assignments, and licensing. Our trademark practice extends to service marks, house marks, certification marks, and collective marks.

Where brand owners face infringement, we coordinate enforcement through the Department of Intellectual Property, Thai Customs, and the Central Intellectual Property and International Trade Court. This includes border measures, customs seizures, and civil litigation to secure injunctive relief and damages.

For companies entering the Thai market, we provide clearance opinions and filing strategies that account for the classification requirements and examination practices of the Thai DIP. For global brands already operating here, we manage renewal portfolios, recordals, and watch services to maintain and defend existing registrations.

What We Handle
Registration and prosecution, opposition and cancellation, enforcement and anti-counterfeiting, customs recordals, licensing and assignments, passing off, IP commercialisation, portfolio management and renewals.

Patent

We advise on patent protection for inventions, industrial designs, and petty patents (utility models) under the Thai Patent Act.

For invention patents, we handle drafting and filing of applications, prosecution through the Department of Intellectual Property, responses to official actions and examiner objections, and maintenance of granted patents through the 20-year term. Thailand offers several mechanisms to accelerate substantive examination — including the ASEAN Patent Examination Co-operation (ASPEC) programme, the Patent Prosecution Highway (PPH) with the Japan Patent Office, and the Target Patent Fast-Track.

For PCT national phase applications, we manage entry into the Thai national phase within the prescribed deadlines, coordinate Thai-language translation of specifications and claims, and prosecute applications through examination.

Design patents protect the ornamental or aesthetic aspects of industrial products, with a 10-year term. Petty patents cover functional innovations that may not meet the inventive step threshold for a full invention patent, with a 6-year initial term extendable to 10 years.

What We Handle
Invention patents, design patents, petty patents, PCT national phase entry, patent prosecution and official action responses, accelerated examination (ASPEC, PPH, Target Patent), annuity management, assignments and licence recordals.

Immigration & Relocation

Our Immigration and Relocation Department advises on all aspects of employing foreign nationals in Thailand — from obtaining work permits and visas before arrival through to long-term residency planning. Since March 2023, the firm has been appointed as a BOI Certified Agency for the Long-Term Resident (LTR) Visa.

Work Permits

We handle new work permit applications, renewals, amendments, and cancellations under both the BOI single-window system and the standard process through the Department of Employment.

For BOI-promoted companies, applications are processed through the Thailand Investment and Expat Services Center (TIESC) using the online single-window system. This covers new work permits (both long-term and short-term), renewals, temporary 30-day permits, and cancellations.

For non-BOI companies, we handle applications through the TIESC or Employment Department directly. This includes the pre-approval (WP3) route, the standard Bor Thor 25 process, renewals, and amendments for changes of workplace or personal particulars.

What We Handle
BOI and non-BOI work permit applications, renewals and amendments, Digital Work Permits (DWP), temporary and urgent work permits, work permit cancellations, change of employer, medical certificate coordination.

Visas

We advise on the full range of visa categories available to foreign nationals working or residing in Thailand.

For most business visa matters, this means the Non-Immigrant B visa — processed simultaneously with the work permit application for employees of BOI-promoted companies, or through the Immigration Bureau for non-BOI companies.

Beyond business visas, we assist with dependent visas for family members, retirement visas, the Smart Visa for qualified professionals in targeted industries, and permanent residency applications. We also handle re-entry permits, 90-day reporting, transfer of visa endorsements, and overseas visa support letters.

Non-Immigrant B and O visas, visa extensions and renewals, dependent visas, retirement visas, Smart Visa, permanent residency, re-entry permits, 90-day reporting, transfer of endorsement (TOE), overseas visa support letters.

Long-Term Resident (LTR) Visa

The LTR Visa is a 10-year visa programme administered by the Board of Investment, designed for high-potential individuals who want to live and work in Thailand long-term. The visa is available to four categories: wealthy global citizens, wealthy pensioners, work-from-Thailand professionals, and highly-skilled professionals.

As a BOI Certified Agency, we manage the entire LTR application process — from initial eligibility assessment through document preparation, submission to the LTR Unit, and obtaining the visa stamp and Digital Work Permit.

The LTR Visa carries significant privileges: a 10-year renewable visa, exemption from the 4:1 Thai-to-foreigner employment ratio, airport fast-track, annual reporting instead of 90-day, reduced income tax of 17% for highly-skilled professionals, and tax exemption on overseas income.

What We Handle
LTR Visa eligibility assessment and pre-screening, application preparation and filing, coordination with the BOI LTR Unit, visa stamp and Digital Work Permit issuance, dependent applications, LTR Visa renewals, annual reporting.

Corporate & Investment

Our Corporate Department advises multinational corporations, joint ventures, SMEs, and entrepreneurs on establishing and operating businesses in Thailand. We handle the legal, regulatory, and administrative requirements across the full business lifecycle.

Market Entry & Formation

We advise on the most appropriate legal structure for entering the Thai market, taking into account the Foreign Business Act, BOI incentives, and the client's commercial objectives.

This includes incorporation of Thai limited companies and public companies, registration of branch offices and representative offices, structuring of joint ventures with Thai partners, and applications for Foreign Business Licences (FBL).

For companies eligible for investment promotion, we prepare and submit applications to the Board of Investment (BOI) and manage the post-approval process including obtaining the BOI promotion certificate and applying for tax and non-tax incentives.

What We Handle
Company incorporation, joint venture structuring, Foreign Business Licence (FBL) applications, BOI promotion applications, IEAT privileges, factory licences, import/export permits, business licensing.

Our Litigation Department handles complex, high-value disputes across Thailand's civil, criminal, and specialised courts — as well as arbitration and mediation proceedings.

Taxation

We advise on all matters relating to taxation in Thailand, covering both the planning and compliance aspects that businesses and individuals encounter when operating in or investing into the country.

On the corporate side, this includes structuring advice on corporate income tax, VAT, customs and excise duties, specific business tax, withholding tax, and transfer pricing. We assist with tax planning for inbound investments, advise on the application of Thailand's double tax treaties (in force with 61 countries), and represent clients in dealings with the Revenue Department.

For individual clients, we advise on personal income tax obligations for expatriates, tax residency issues, and the tax implications of various visa categories — including the reduced 17% flat rate available under the LTR Visa programme.

Our tax practice works in close coordination with the corporate and immigration departments, particularly on BOI-promoted investments where tax incentives are central to the investment structure.

What We Handle
Corporate income tax, VAT advisory, customs and excise, withholding tax, transfer pricing, double tax treaties, tax audit defence, personal income tax for expatriates, BOI tax incentives, cross-border tax structuring.
