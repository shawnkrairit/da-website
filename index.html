Practice Area

Intellectual Property

Our IP Department handles every stage of intellectual property protection in Thailand and across the region — from initial filing and registration through to enforcement, licensing, and commercialisation.

Trademark

We advise on the full lifecycle of trademark protection under Thai law: clearance searches, filing, prosecution, opposition and cancellation proceedings, renewals, assignments, and licensing.

Where brand owners face infringement, we coordinate enforcement through the Department of Intellectual Property, Thai Customs, and the Central Intellectual Property and International Trade Court.

For companies entering the Thai market, we provide clearance opinions and filing strategies that account for the classification requirements and examination practices of the Thai DIP.

What We Handle
Registration and prosecution, opposition and cancellation, enforcement and anti-counterfeiting, customs recordals, licensing and assignments, passing off, IP commercialisation, portfolio management and renewals.

Patent

We advise on patent protection for inventions, industrial designs, and petty patents (utility models) under the Thai Patent Act.

For invention patents, we handle drafting and filing of applications, prosecution through the DIP, responses to official actions, and maintenance of granted patents through the 20-year term. Thailand offers several mechanisms to accelerate examination — including ASPEC, the Patent Prosecution Highway (PPH) with the Japan Patent Office, and the Target Patent Fast-Track.

For PCT national phase applications, we manage entry into the Thai national phase, coordinate Thai-language translation, and prosecute applications through examination.

What We Handle
Invention patents, design patents, petty patents, PCT national phase entry, patent prosecution, accelerated examination (ASPEC, PPH, Target Patent), annuity management, assignments and licence recordals.

Immigration

Our Immigration Department advises on all aspects of employing foreign nationals in Thailand. Since March 2023, we are a BOI Certified Agency for the Long-Term Resident (LTR) Visa.

Work Permits

We handle new work permit applications, renewals, amendments, and cancellations under both the BOI single-window system and the standard process through the Department of Employment.

For BOI-promoted companies, applications are processed through TIESC using the online single-window system. For non-BOI companies, we handle applications through TIESC or the Employment Department directly.

What We Handle
BOI and non-BOI work permit applications, renewals and amendments, Digital Work Permits (DWP), temporary and urgent work permits, work permit cancellations, change of employer, medical certificate coordination.

Long-Term Resident (LTR) Visa

The LTR Visa is a 10-year visa programme administered by the BOI, available to four categories: wealthy global citizens, wealthy pensioners, work-from-Thailand professionals, and highly-skilled professionals.

As a BOI Certified Agency, we manage the entire application process — from eligibility assessment through document preparation, submission, and obtaining the visa stamp and Digital Work Permit.

What We Handle
LTR Visa eligibility assessment and pre-screening, application preparation and filing, coordination with the BOI LTR Unit, visa stamp and Digital Work Permit issuance, dependent applications, renewals, annual reporting.

Practice Area

Litigation, Mediation & ADR

Our Litigation Department handles complex, high-value disputes across Thailand's civil, criminal, and specialised courts — as well as arbitration and mediation.

Civil & Commercial Litigation

We represent clients from the Courts of First Instance through to the Supreme Court, handling high-value commercial disputes, contract claims, shareholder disputes, and debt recovery proceedings. Our team has particular depth in employment matters, IP and international trade cases, and tax disputes before the specialised courts.

For foreign clients unfamiliar with the Thai court system, we provide clear guidance on procedure, realistic assessments of timeline and cost, and strategic advice on whether litigation is the most effective path to resolution.

What We Handle
Civil and commercial litigation, shareholder and joint venture disputes, contract enforcement, debt recovery, employment and labour disputes, IP infringement proceedings, international trade court matters, tax court disputes, criminal defence for business-related offences, bankruptcy and restructuring.

Domestic & International Arbitration

For disputes involving international parties or cross-border transactions, arbitration is often the preferred forum. We handle matters before the Thai Arbitration Institute (TAI), the Thailand Arbitration Center (THAC), and advise on proceedings under ICC, SIAC, and HKIAC rules.

We also advise on the enforcement of foreign arbitral awards in Thailand under the New York Convention, and assist foreign clients seeking to enforce Thai arbitral awards in other jurisdictions. Our approach prioritises efficiency and cost-effectiveness while protecting our clients' commercial interests.

What We Handle
Domestic arbitration (TAI, THAC), international arbitration (ICC, SIAC, HKIAC rules), enforcement of foreign arbitral awards under the New York Convention, arbitration clause drafting and review, interim measures and emergency arbitration, investor-state disputes.

Mediation & Alternative Dispute Resolution

Not every dispute requires a courtroom. We advise clients on mediation and other forms of alternative dispute resolution where a negotiated settlement is more practical, cost-effective, or commercially desirable than formal proceedings.

Our team acts as counsel in mediation proceedings and also assists in structuring settlement agreements that are enforceable across jurisdictions. For ongoing commercial relationships where preserving the partnership matters, mediation often achieves better outcomes than adversarial processes.

What We Handle
Court-annexed mediation, private mediation, negotiated settlements, dispute prevention advisory, settlement agreement drafting, multi-party dispute coordination, pre-litigation negotiation strategy.

Notarial Services

Licensed Notarial Services Attorneys providing full notarial services for domestic and international clients under the Lawyers Act B.E. 2528.

Document Certification & Notarisation

Our licensed Notarial Services Attorneys certify and notarise documents for use in Thailand and foreign jurisdictions. This includes certification of true copies, notarisation of signatures, powers of attorney, and sworn statements required for corporate filings, immigration applications, or cross-border transactions.

We regularly handle bulk notarisation for multinational companies requiring certified documents across multiple jurisdictions simultaneously.

What We Handle
Certification of document copies, notarisation of signatures, powers of attorney for foreign jurisdictions, sworn statements and statutory declarations, corporate document certification for government filings.

Signature Authentication & Oaths

We provide signature verification and authentication services, administration of oaths, and identity verification for individuals and corporate representatives. These services are essential for foreign nationals executing legal documents in Thailand or Thai nationals preparing documents for use abroad.

Our office can accommodate urgent requests and high-volume requirements for corporate clients with regular notarisation needs.

What We Handle
Signature verification and authentication, recording of statements under oath, administration of oaths, identity verification, witness attestation, apostille coordination for documents used internationally.
